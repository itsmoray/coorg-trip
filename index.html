24TH · WEDKannur sights → Coorg

A morning in Kannur, then up to the hills

Kannur sightseeing 8:30 AM–12 PM · then ~115 km drive to Madikeri
8:30 – 9:30 AM

St. Angelo Fort 1 hr

1505 Portuguese fort with laterite walls, cannons, and sweeping Arabian Sea views. Easy walk, good for the whole family.

₹25 entry
9:35 – 10:00 AM

Mappila Bay 25 min

Fishing harbour right next to the fort — boats, nets, and a nice sea breeze. Quick stop, drive right up to it.

₹40 for car entry
10:15 – 11:00 AM

Payyambalam Beach & Park 45 min

Kannur's main beach with a walkable promenade and sculpture park. Good for a relaxed stroll before the long drive.

₹20 park entry
11:00 AM – 12:00 PM

Lunch in Kannur 1 hr

Eat a proper meal before setting off — next solid food stop will likely be on the road.

12:15 PM

Depart Kannur → Madikeri

3–3.5 hrs via NH75 through the Western Ghats. Hairpin bends — better to drive this stretch in daylight.

~3:00 – 3:45 PM

Abbi Waterfall 45 min

70-ft falls reached by a short walk through coffee plantations, right on the way into Madikeri.

₹30 entry
5:30 – 6:30 PM

Raja's Seat — sunset 1 hr

Madikeri's iconic viewpoint over the valley. Garden, play area, ziplining if the kids want activity time. Arrive before sunset, it fills up fast.

Don't miss
Evening

Overnight in Madikeri Sleep

Plenty of resorts/homestays in town — happy to shortlist specific names if you want.

⏱ Buffer: Kannur sightseeing block has light spacing built in for traffic/parking. If you finish early, you can linger at the beach instead of rushing to Coorg.
25TH · THUCoorg → drive home overnight

A full day in Coorg, then the long drive back

Sightseeing 8 AM–6 PM · depart by 9–10 PM · reach Anchal ~5–6 AM Fri
6:00 – 7:30 AM

Mandalpatti viewpoint 1.5 hr

Jeep-only road, best misty sunrise views in Coorg. Only for early risers — needs a 6 AM start and a 4x4/jeep hire at the base.

Optional, early start
9:00 – 10:00 AM

Mountain View Coffee Plantation 1 hr

Guided walk through coffee & spice plants, ends with coffee/wine tasting.

~₹200/person
10:30 – 11:00 AM

Sri Omkareshwar Temple 30 min

1820s Shiva temple with an unusual Indo-Saracenic dome-and-minaret look, calm pond out front.

12:30 – 2:30 PM

Dubare Elephant Camp 2 hr

~40 min from Madikeri. Watch or join elephant bathing/feeding sessions with a naturalist guide — a highlight for kids.

Entry + activity fees vary
3:15 – 4:15 PM

Namdroling Golden Temple, Bylakuppe 1 hr

Tibetan Buddhist monastery with a massive golden Buddha statue and vivid architecture — calm and visually stunning, ~1 hr from Dubare.

5:00 – 6:00 PM

Dinner in Madikeri 1 hr

Early dinner before the long overnight drive — last proper sit-down meal before the road.

9:00 – 10:00 PM

Depart Coorg → Anchal

~430–450 km via Kozhikode and NH66 south. Driving overnight means no daytime traffic, but plan driver swaps/rest stops every 2 hrs.

~5:00 – 6:00 AM (Fri)

Arrive Anchal

End of the trip — a quiet early morning roll back home.

🛣 Overnight drive note: the Madikeri ghat descent should ideally be done before full dark if possible — leaving by 8:30–9 PM gives a buffer. Keep the driver fresh with a coffee stop in Kozhikode around midnight.
